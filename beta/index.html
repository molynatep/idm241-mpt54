IDM 241 - Alpha Build

Yelp Microinteraction - Link

Trigger:

  • Hover over photo
  • Click on photo

Rules:

  • Photo expands
  • Drop shadow appears
  • New photo fades in on top of current photo after 2 seconds
  • Cursor changes to pointer
  • Photo goes back to original size when hovered off photo
  • Drop shadow disappears when hovered off photo
  • Remain on same photo the slideshow was on when hovered off photo
  • When the photo is clicked on a modal pops up with a large image of what was clicked on with more photos and reviews on the righthand side
  • The modal slides up from the bottom and a black background with 50% opacity fades in behind the modal
  • When x is clicked on the modal, the background fades out back to 0% opacity and the modal slides off the screen towards the bottom

Feedback:

  • Photo expands
  • Drop shadow appears
  • New photo fades in on top of current photo after 2 seconds
  • Cursor changes to pointer
  • Photo goes back to original size when hovered off photo
  • Drop shadow disappears when hovered off photo
  • Remain on same photo the slideshow was on when hovered off photo
  • When the photo is clicked on a modal pops up with a large image of what was clicked on with more photos and reviews on the righthand side
  • The modal slides up from the bottom and a black background with 50% opacity fades in behind the modal
  • When x is clicked on the modal, the background fades out back to 0% opacity and the modal slides off the screen towards the bottom

Loop & Modes:

  • Slideshow repeats until user is no longer hovering over the photo
  • When user clicks on the image, a modal comes up on the screen and closes when the x is clicked on
